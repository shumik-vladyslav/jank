* Data Breach Risk Model:

The computed risk score is a measure of the probability that your system will be breached in routine attacks by hackers on the internet.

This model takes into account the fact that the perimeter security and access controls will have temporary gaps leading to data exposures.

The computed risk score is a simplified measure of the qualitative data in-security in the enterprise systems.

The higher the risk score, the higher the risk of data breach and sensitive data loss.

Threshold for acceptable Risk Score needs to be determined by the data owners in organizations.

Any Risk Score > 0 may introduce non-compliance with International, Federal or State Information Privacy Regulations.
